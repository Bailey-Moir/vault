*Buses* bus information around. There are three types:
- **Data Buses**
  Bidirectional *buses* that carry actual data.
 - **Address Buses**
   *Buses* that specify memory locations, usually controlled by the [[CPU|CPU]].
- **Control Buses**
   *Buses* that specify the direction of other *buses*, and when data transfer occurs.
