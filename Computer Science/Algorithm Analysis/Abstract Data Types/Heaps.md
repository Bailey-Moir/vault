A *min/max heap* is a [[Computer Science/Algorithm Analysis/Abstract Data Types/Trees/Trees|tree]] such that [[Nodes|nodes']] keys are *smaller/larger* than (or equal to) the keys of their [[Parent, Children, and Sibling Nodes|children]]. Therefore, the *min/max* of a *min/max heap* is the [[Root|root]].
