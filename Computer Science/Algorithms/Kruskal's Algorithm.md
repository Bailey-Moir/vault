*Kruskal's algorithm* finds a [[Spanning Trees|minimum spanning tree]] by placing all [[Math/Discrete/Graph Theory/Definitions/Edges|edges]] in a [[Priority Queues|minimum priority queue]], adding them to the [[Math/Discrete/Graph Theory/Definitions/Trees|tree]] if they don't create a [[Walks|cycle]].
$$\mathcal{O}(\left| E \right| \log \left|  E \right|  )$$
