The *minhash* of $S \subseteq U$ for some [[Universal Set|universal set]] $U$ is the [[Expected Value|expected]] number of times you need to choose a random element of $U$ without replacement until you choose an element in $S$.

> [!note] In the [[Characteristic Polynomial|characteristic matrix]], this is expected first $1$ row of $S$ after shuffling the order of $U$.
