In the *[[Graphs|graph]] traversal* process, some (or all) of the [[Vertices|vertices]] go through the following stages (in order):
- **Undiscovered**
  The [[Vertices|vertex]] has not been encountered yet.
- **Unprocessed**
  The [[Vertices|vertex]] has been encountered, but not all [[Vertex Neighbourhoods|neighbours]] have been discovered.
- **Processed**
  All [[Vertex Neighbourhoods|neighbours]] of the [[Vertices|vertex]] have been discovered.
