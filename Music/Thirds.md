A *major/minor third* is the [[Music/Intervals|interval]] between $\text{I}$ and $\text{III}$ in a major/minor [[Scales|scale]]. 
- A *major third* is an [[Music/Intervals|interval]] of 5 [[Tones & Semitones|semitones]].
- A *minor third* is an [[Music/Intervals|interval]] of 4 [[Tones & Semitones|semitones]].
