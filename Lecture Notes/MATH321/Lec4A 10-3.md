addition cosets are like integration constants. e.g.
$$a + R = b + R \iff a - b \in R$$
cause
$$\begin{align}
	a + R &= b + R\\
	\to a &\in b + R\\
	a - b &\in R\\
\end{align}$$
and
$$\begin{align}
	a - b &\in R \\
	a - b &= r \\
	a &= b + r \\
	\to a + R &= b + R \\
\end{align}$$
