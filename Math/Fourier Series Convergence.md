Suppose that $f$ is [[Periodic Functions|periodic]], and that both $f$ and $f'$ are [[Piecewise Continuity|piecewise continuous]]. Then
$$\lim_{ N \to \infty } s_{N}(t) = f^*(t)$$
where $f^*(t) = \frac{1}{2}\left( \lim_{ x \to t^{-} } f(x) + \lim_{ x \to t^+ } f(x) \right)$.
