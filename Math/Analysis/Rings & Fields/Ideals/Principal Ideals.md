Let $a \in R$, where $R$ is a [[Groups#^abelian-groups|commutative]] [[Rings|ring]] with [[Rings#^unity|unity]]. The [[Sets|set]]
$$\left< a \right> = Ra = \{ ra : r \in R \},$$
is an [[Ideals|ideal]] called the *principal ideal* generated by $a$.

V NEVERMIND THIS IS WRONG. 
> [!theorem] $\left< a \right>$  is the smallest [[Subrings|subring]] of $R$ containing $a$.
