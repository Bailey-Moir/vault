Let $F$ be a [[Fields|field]], and $\alpha \in E$ for some [[Subfields#^extension-field|extension field]] $E$ of $F$.
We call $\alpha$ *algebraic* over $F$ iff $\alpha$ is a root of a non-zero [[Polynomial Rings|polynomial]] in $F[x]$.

> [!definition] Otherwise, $\alpha$ is called **transcendental**.
^transcendetal
