Let $R$ be a [[Rings|ring]] **with [[Rings#^unity|unity]]**, and $n = \char R$.
> $R$ has a [[Subrings|subring]] [[Isomorphisms|isomorphic]] to $\Z_{n}$ if $n > 0$, and $\Z$ otherwise (if $n = 0$).

Let $F$ be a [[Fields|field]], and $p = \char R$.
> $F$ has a [[Subrings|subring]] [[Isomorphisms|isomorphic]] to $\Z_{p}$ if $p > 0$, and $\Q$ otherwise (if $p = 0$).
