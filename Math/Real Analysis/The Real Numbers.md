The real numbers, $\mathbb{R}$, is a mathematical object / space defined by 4 axioms:
1. $\mathbb{R}$ is a [[Fields|field]] (support arithmetic).
2. $\mathbb{R}$ is ordered (basis for topology of $\mathbb{R}$).
3. $\mathbb{R}$ is an [[Ordered Fields|ordered field]] (interaction of order and algebra).
4. $\mathbb{R}$ is complete (finishes the topological structure).
