$$ A = \begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d\end{bmatrix} \implies A^{-1} = \dfrac{1}{\left|A\right|} \begin{bmatrix} d & -b \\ -c & a \end{bmatrix}$$
The matrix is transposed along the secondary diagonal, and this same diagonal then changes sign, divided by the [[Determinant of a 2x2 Matrix|determinant]].
