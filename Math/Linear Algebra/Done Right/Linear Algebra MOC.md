Linear algebra is the study of [[Linear Maps|linear maps]] on finite-dimensional [[Vector Spaces|vector spaces]].

For these notes,
- $\mathbb{F}$ denotes $\mathbb{R}$, $\mathbb{C}$, or any other arbitrary [[Fields|field]].
- $V$ denotes a [[Vector Spaces|vector space]] over $\mathbb{F}$.
