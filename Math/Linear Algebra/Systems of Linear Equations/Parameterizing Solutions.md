> [!example]
> $$\begin{bmatrix}
> 	1 & 1 & 1 & -1 & : & 2 \\
> 	0 & 0 & 2 & 2 & : & 0 \\
> \end{bmatrix}$$
> Pivots: $x$ and $z$.
> Free variables: $y$ and $w$
> One parameter for each free variable.
> let $y = s$, $w = t$, $s,t \in \R$.
> $$\begin{align*}
> 	2z + 2t &= 0 \\
> 	z &= -t
> \end{align*}$$
> $$\begin{align*}
> 	x + s - t - t &= 2 \\
> 	x &= 2 + 2t - s
> \end{align*}$$
