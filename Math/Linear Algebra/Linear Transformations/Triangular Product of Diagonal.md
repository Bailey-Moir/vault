A [[Matrices|matrix]] of the form $\begin{bmatrix}a_{11}&a_{12}&a_{13}\\0&a_{22}&a_{23}\\0&0&a_{33}\end{bmatrix}$ has a determinant of $a_{11}a_{22}a_{33}$;
$$\left|\begin{matrix}a_{11}&a_{12}&a_{13}\\0&a_{22}&a_{23}\\0&0&a_{33}\end{matrix}\right| = a_{11}\times a_{22}\times a_{33}$$
