For an [[Operators|operator]] $T \in \mathcal{L}(V)$ An [[Subspaces|subspace]] $U$ of $V$ is a *invariant* under $T$ iff
$$\forall \vecbf{u} \in U, T\,\vecbf{u} \in U$$
> [!examples]-
> - $\text{null}\,T$[^1]
> - $\text{range}\,T$[^2]
> - $\{ \vecbf{0} \}$
> - $V$

[^1]: [[Null Space]]
[^2]: [[Range & Image]]
