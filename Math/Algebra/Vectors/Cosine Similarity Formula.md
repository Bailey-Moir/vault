From the cosine definition of the [[Dot Product|dot product]], it can be shown that if $\theta$ is the angle between two non-zero vectors $\mathbf{\underline{a}}$ and $\mathbf{\underline{b}}$, then:
$$ \cos \theta = \dfrac{\mathbf{\underline{a}} \cdot \mathbf{\underline{b}}}{|\mathbf{\underline{a}}|\,|\mathbf{\underline{b}}|} $$
