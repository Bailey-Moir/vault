$$ \mathbf{\underline{a}} \:\bot\: \mathbf{\underline{b}} \iff \mathbf{\underline{a}} \cdot \mathbf{\underline{b}} = 0  $$

> [!proof] $\bot$ Proof
> $$ \begin{align*}
> 	\mathbf{\underline{a}} \cdot \mathbf{\underline{b}} &= \left|\mathbf{\underline{a}}\right| \left|\mathbf{\underline{b}}\right| \cos \theta \\
> 	&= \left|\mathbf{\underline{a}}\right| \left|\mathbf{\underline{b}}\right| \cos \left( \pm\dfrac{\pi}{2} \right) && \text{Perpendicular} \\
> 	&= \left|\mathbf{\underline{a}}\right| \left|\mathbf{\underline{b}}\right| (0) \\
> 	&= 0 \\
> \end{align*}$$
