## Remainder Estimate for the Integral Test
$$ R_n = s_\infty - s_n = a_{n+1} + a_{n+2} + \cdots $$
If $a_n = f(n)$ and $f$ is a [[Definition of Continuity|continuous]], positive, [[Increasing and Decreasing Functions|decreasing]] function on $[N,\infty)$ and $\sum a_n$ is convergent (which can be checked using the integral test, then:
$$ \int_{N+1}^\infty f(x) \, dx \le R_N \le \int_N^\infty f(x) \, dx $$
