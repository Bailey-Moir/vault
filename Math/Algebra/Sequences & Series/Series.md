$$\begin{array}{cc}
	\begin{array}{l}
		s_1 = a_1 \\
		s_2 = a_1 + a_2 \\
		s_3 = a_1 + a_2 + a_3 \\
		s_4 = a_1 + a_2 + a_3 + a_4 \\
		\cdots
	\end{array}
	&
	\begin{array}{rcl}
		s_n = \displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^n a_i
	\end{array}
\end{array}$$
Partial sums make up their own series $\{s_n\}$, which may or may not have a limit.
$$\LARGE \sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n = \lim_{n\to\infty} s_n$$
