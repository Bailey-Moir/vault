 If $f$ is a [[Definition of Continuity|continuous]], positive, [[Monotonic Sequences|monotonically decreasing]] [[Extending Sequences|extension]] of $\left\{a_n\right\}$^[[[Sequences]]] on $[N,\infty)$^[[[Intervals]]], then 
 - $\displaystyle\sum_{n=N}^\infty a_n$[^1] is convergent iff $\displaystyle\int_N^\infty f(x) \, dx$[^2] is convergent.

[^1]: [[Series]]
[^2]: [[Improper Integrals]]
