Let $\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^\infty a_k$ be a [[Series|series]] with positive terms, and let $\displaystyle\rho = \lim_{k\to\infty} \dfrac{a_{k+1}}{a_k}$ (the limit of the ratio between terms).
- If $\rho < 1$, the series converges.
- If $\rho = 1$, the test is inconclusive.
- If $\rho > 1$, the series diverges.
