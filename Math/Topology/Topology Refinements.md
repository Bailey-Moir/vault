If $\mathcal{T}_{1}$ and $\mathcal{T}_{2}$ are [[Topology|topologies]] on $X$, we say that $\mathcal{T_{1}}$ is a *refinement* of $\mathcal{T}_{2}$ iff $\mathcal{T}_{2}\subseteq \mathcal{T}_{1}$
(iff every [[Open Sets|open set]] on $\mathcal{T}_{2}$ is an [[Open Sets|open set]] on $\mathcal{T}_{1}$).

> [!definition] Given two [[Topology|topologies]] on $X$, if neither *refines* the other, they are said to be **incomparable topologies**.
