Two [[Topology|topological spaces]] $X$ and $Y$ are *homeomorphic* iff there is a [[Homeomorphisms|homeomorphism]] between them.

> [!notation] This is denoted as $X \cong Y$, or $X \overset{\varphi}\cong Y$ where $\varphi$ is a [[Homeomorphisms|homeomorphism]] between $X$ and $Y$.
