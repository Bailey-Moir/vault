An *$\mathit{\varepsilon}$-ball* around a [[Vectors|vector]] $\vecbf{x} \in \R^n$ is
$$B_{\varepsilon}(\vecbf{x}) = \left\{ \vecbf{y} \in \R^n : d(\vecbf{x},\vecbf{y}) < \varepsilon \right\},$$
where $d(\vecbf{x},\vecbf{y})$ is the specified [[Norms|norm]], $\| \vecbf{x} - \vecbf{y} \|$.
