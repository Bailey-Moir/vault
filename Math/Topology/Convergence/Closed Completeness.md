Let $E \subseteq M$ for some [[Metric Spaces|metric space]] $\left( M,d \right)$. Then
- If $E$ is [[Completeness|complete]], $E$ is [[Closed Sets|closed]], and
- If $E$ is [[Closed Sets|closed]] and $X$ is [[Completeness|complete]], then $E$ is [[Completeness|complete]].
