A [[Subsets|subset]] $S \subseteq X$ of a [[Topology|topological space]] $\left( X,\mathcal{T} \right)$ is an *open set* iff $S \in \mathcal{T}$.

> [!theorem] A [[Sets|set]] $S\subseteq\R^n$ is *open* with the usual [[Topology|topology]] iff $\forall x \in S, \exists\varepsilon>0 : B_{\varepsilon}(x) \subseteq S$[^1].

[^1]: [[Epsilon Balls]]
