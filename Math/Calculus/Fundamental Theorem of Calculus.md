If $f \in \mathcal{C}([a,b])$, then:
1. $\displaystyle \dfrac{d}{dx} \int_a^x f(t) \, dt = f(x) \hspace{8em} x \in [a,b]$
2. $\left( \forall x \in \left[ a,b \right], \displaystyle\int_a^x f(t) \, dt = F(x) - F(a)  \right)\iff \left( \forall x \in \left[ a,b \right], F'(x) = f(x) \right)$
