$$
f \in \mathcal{C}\left(\left\{\lim_{x\to a} g(x) \right\}\right) \iff \lim_{x\to a} f(g(x)) = f\left(\lim_{x\to a} g(x)\right)
$$
This is derived from the [[Math/Calculus/Limits/Continuity/Continuity|definition of continuity]], where the [[Intervals|interval]] in question is $\displaystyle \lim_{x\to a} g(x)$.
