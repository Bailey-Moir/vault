The *[[Unit Vectors|unit]] normal [[Vectors|vector]]* of $\vecbf{r}(t)$ is the [[Unit Vectors|unit vector]] of the [[Derivatives of Vector-Valued Functions|derivative]] of its [[Unit Tangent Vectors|unit tangent vector]]:
$$\vecbf{N}(t) = \dfrac{\vecbf{T}'(t)}{\| \vecbf{T}'(t) \|}$$
- $\forall t, \vecbf{N}(t) \:\bot\: \vecbf{T}(t)$^[[[Orthogonal Vectors]]]
- $\vecbf{N}(t)$ is to $\vecbf{T}(t)$ what $\vecbf{T}(t)$ is to $\vecbf{r}(t)$^[[[Unit Tangent Vectors]]].
