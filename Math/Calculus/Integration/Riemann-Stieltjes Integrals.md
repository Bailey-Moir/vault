The *[[Riemann-Stieltjes Sums#Upper and Lower Riemann-Stieltjes Sums|upper Riemann-Stieltjes]] integral* is defined by
$$\overline{\int_{a}^b} f \, dg = \inf\{ \overline{S}(P;f,g) : P \text{ is a partition of } \left[ a,b \right] \}$$
The *[[Riemann-Stieltjes Sums#Upper and Lower Riemann-Stieltjes Sums|lower Riemann-Stieltjes]] integral* is defined by
$$\underline{\int_{a}^b} f \, dg = \sup\{ \underline{S}(P;f,g) : P \text{ is a partition of } \left[ a,b \right] \}$$
> [!definition] We say that $f$ is [[Math/Calculus/Integration/Integrability|integrable]] with respect to $g$ on $\left[ a,b \right]$ iff the upper and lower *R-S integrals* are equal.
