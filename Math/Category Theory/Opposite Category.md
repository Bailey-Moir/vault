The *opposite category* $\mathcal{C}^\text{op}$ of a [[Categories|category]] $\mathcal{C}$:
- has the same [[Categories|object]] [[Classes|class]],
- the same [[Composite Functions|composition]] [[Functions|function]], but
- for all $x,y \in \mathcal{C}$, $\mathcal{C}(x,y) = \mathcal{C}^\text{op}(y,x)$.
