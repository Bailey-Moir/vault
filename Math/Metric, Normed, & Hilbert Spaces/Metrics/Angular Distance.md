The *angular distance* (called *cosine distance* in DATA301 for some reason) between two [[Vectors|vectors]] $\vecbf{u},\vecbf{v} \in V$ in an [[Inner Products|inner product space]] gives the angle between the two [[Vectors|vectors]], i.e.
$$d(\vecbf{u},\vecbf{v}) = \theta = \arccos \left( \dfrac{\vecbf{u} \cdot \vecbf{v}}{\| \vecbf{u} \|\| \vecbf{v} \|} \right)$$
by the [[Cosine Similarity Formula|cosine similarity formula]].
