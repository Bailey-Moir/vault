A [[Sequences|sequence]] of [[Functions|functions]] $\left( f_{n} \right)$ in $\R^{X}$[^1] *point-wise converges* to $g \in \R^X$[^1] iff
$$\forall x \in X, \lim_{ n \to \infty } f_{n}(x) = g(x),$$
> [!notation] Expanding [[Metric Convergence|Metric Convergence]] Definition$$ \forall x \in X, \forall \varepsilon > 0, \exists N \in \N : \forall n \ge N, \left| f_{n}(x) - g(x) \right| < \varepsilon. $$

[^1]: [[Sets of Functions]]
