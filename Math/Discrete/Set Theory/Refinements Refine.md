If $P$ and $Q$ are [[Interval Partitions|partitions]] of $\left[ a,b \right]$, and $Q$ is a [[Refinements|refinement]] of $P$, then
$$\underline{S}(P;f,g) \le \underline{S}(Q;f,g) \le \overline{S}(Q;f,g) \le \overline{S}(P;f,g).$$
> [!proof]-
