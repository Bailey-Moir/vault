The *domain* of a [[Relations|relation]] $\rel \in A \times B$^[[[Cartesian Product]]] is the [[Sets|set]]
$$ \left\{ a \in A : \exists b \in B: (a,b) \in \rel \right\} $$
If $\mathcal{R}$ is a [[Functions|function]], this must necessarily be equal to $A$.
