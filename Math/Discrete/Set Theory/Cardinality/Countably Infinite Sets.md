A [[Sets|set]] is *countably infinite* iff it is in the [[Equivalence Classes|equivalence class]] $\left[\left|\Z^+\right|\right]$[^1].
- A [[Sets|set]] is **countable** if it is finite or *countably infinite*.

> [!note] $\Q$ is countable

[^1]: [[Cardinality]]
