*Classes* are extensions of [[Sets|sets]], meaning every [[Sets|set]] is a *class*. A *class* that cannot be a [[Sets|set]] is called a **proper class**. The key property of *classes* is

> *Classes* cannot contain **proper classes**.

This eliminates paradoxes such as [[Russell's Paradox|Russel's Paradox]].
