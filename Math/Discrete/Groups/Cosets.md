Let $H$ be a [[Subgroups|subgroup]] of $G$. A (left) *coset* of $H$ in $G$ is a [[Subsets|subset]] of $G$ of the form
$$g*H = \{ g*h : h \in H \}$$
for some $g \in G$.

> [!example] $a \left< b \right> = \{ a*b^n : m \in \Z \}$
