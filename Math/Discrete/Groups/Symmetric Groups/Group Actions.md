An *action* of a [[Groups|group]] on a [[Sets|set]] $S$ is a [[Functions|function]] $G\times S \to S$[^1], written $(g,s)\mapsto g(s)$ satisfying
- $e(s) = s$
- $\forall g,h \in G,s \in S, \left( gh(S) = g(h(S)) \right)$

> [!example] $G = S_{n}$[^2] acts on $S = \{ 1,\dots,n \}$.

[^1]: [[Cartesian Product]]

[^2]: [[Symmetric Groups]]
