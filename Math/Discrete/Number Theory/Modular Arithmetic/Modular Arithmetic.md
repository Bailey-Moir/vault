- **Addition in $\mathbf{\Z_n}$**[^1] 
$a + b = c$ where $c$ is the *principal remainder* when $a+b$ is divided by $n$.
- **Multiplication in $\mathbf{\Z_n}$**[^1] 
$ab = c$ where $c$ is the *principal remainder* when $ab$ is divided by $n$.
