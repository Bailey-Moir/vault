A *spanning tree* of a [[Connectivitiy|connected]] [[Math/Discrete/Graph Theory/Graphs|graph]] is a [[Subgraphs|sub]][[Math/Discrete/Graph Theory/Definitions/Trees|tree]] formed from the same [[Vertices|vertices]] and a [[Subsets|subset]] of the [[Math/Discrete/Graph Theory/Definitions/Edges|edges]] with minimum total [[Weighted Graphs|weight]].
- If the graph is [[Weighted Graphs|unweighted]], then a minimum number of edges.
