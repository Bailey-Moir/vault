Two [[Statements and Propositions|statements]] are **logically equivalent** if and only if they have the same [[Truth Tables|truth table]].
If $p$ and $q$ are **logically equivalent**, we write $p \iff q$.

> [!example]
> $$p\to q \iff \neg q \to \neg p$$
> \[[[Converse, Inverse, and Contrapositive|contrapositive]]\]
