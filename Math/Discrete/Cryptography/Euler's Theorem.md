Let $n \in \Z^+$ and $a \in \Z$. If $\gcd(a,n) = 1$^[[[Greatest Common Divisor]]], then
$$ a^{\phi(n)} \equiv 1 \pmod n $$
This is a generalization of [[Fermat's Little Theorem]], as can be seen by letting $n \in \mathbb{P}$.
