$$p \to q \iff \neg q \to \neg p$$
You can prove $p \to q$ by proving the [[Converse, Inverse, and Contrapositive|contrapositive]] ($\neg q \to \neg p$), as the two are [[Logical Equivalence|logically equivalent]].
