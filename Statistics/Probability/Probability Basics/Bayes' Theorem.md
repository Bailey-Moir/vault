$$\begin{align*}
	P(A|B) &= \dfrac{P(B|A)P(A)}{P(B)} \\
	P(A|B) &= \dfrac{P(A)}{P(B|A)P(A) + P(B|A')P(A')}P(B|A) && (1) \\
\end{align*}$$
This can be derived from the [[Conditional Probability|conditional probability formula]].
