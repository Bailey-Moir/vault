For a [[Binomial Distribution|binomial distribution]] $\text{Bin}(n,p)$, the [[Mean|mean]] is $np$ and the [[Variance of a Random Variable|variance]] is $np(1-p)$.
$$ X \sim \text{Bin}(n,p) \implies \begin{array}{l} 
	E(X) = np \\
	\sigma^2 = np(1-p)
\end{array} $$
