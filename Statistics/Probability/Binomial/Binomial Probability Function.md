For a [[Binomial Random Variable|binomial random variable]] $X$ with $n$ trials and [[Probabilities|probability]] of success $p$ on each trial, the probability of exactly $k$ successes in the $n$ trials is given by:
$$ P(X=k) = \begin{pmatrix} n \\ k \end{pmatrix} p^k (1-p)^\left(n-k\right) $$
