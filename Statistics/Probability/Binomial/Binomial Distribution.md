Conditions:
1. Fixed number $n$ of trials, each with two outcomes (success, $S$, and failure, $F$)
2. Fixed [[Probabilities|probability]] $p$ of $S$ for each trial.
3. Outcomes of the trials are [[Independent Events|independent]].

If $X = \text{\# of } S \text{ in } n \text{ trials}$ then $X \sim \text{Bin}(n,p)$.
